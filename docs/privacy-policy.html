Core Commitment: SimRun is a fully local application. All your data is stored only on your device. We do not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

SimRun is a locally-run running tracking app. All features operate entirely on your device. We:

  • âŒ Do not require account registration
  • âŒ Do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • âŒ Do not upload your running data to any server
  • âŒ Do not use any third-party analytics tools
  • âŒ Do not display advertisements

2. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • GPS running route data
  • Workout statistics (distance, duration, pace, calories)
  • App settings (language, theme, units, etc.)
  • Optional: User avatar and nickname (for local display only)

3. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to provide core features:

  • Location: Used to record running routes. Location data is saved locally only and never uploaded.
  • Motion Sensors: Used for step counting. Data is used locally only.
  • Photo Library (Optional): Only used for selecting avatar images. Not uploaded.
  • iCloud (Optional): Only used when you enable cloud sync to back up data to your iCloud account.

4. Data Export and Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Support exporting running records as GPX, JSON, or CSV format
  • Delete: You can delete individual records or clear all data at any time
  • Backup: Optional backup to iCloud or local storage only

5. Third-Party Services

SimRun uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple MapKit / Google Maps: Used for map display (subject to their respective privacy policies)
  • iCloud CloudKit: Only used when cloud sync is enabled. Data is stored in your Apple account.

6. Children's Privacy

SimRun is suitable for users aged 4 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be announced in the app and on this page.
